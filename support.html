Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gob Goblet free?

Yes, completely.

Does it work offline?

Partially — encryption and decryption of the file content requires the key service for the inner layer. Outer layer decryption (metadata reveal) works completely offline.

What happens if I lose my password?

The file cannot be recovered. Gob Goblet considers the offering permanent.
